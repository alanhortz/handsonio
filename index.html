THE VALUES OF THE MOVEMENT

COLLABORATION

We value collaborative behaviors.

INNOVATION

We believe that mastering different technologies favours innovation.

SOLUTION

We believe that the search for a solution to a given problem may lead to the finding of a solution to a different problem.

RESPECT

We respect the time of everyone and we try hard to clearly establish the benefits of the demonstrated technologies.

EVOLUTION

We believe that personal evolution is a key component of the team dynamic.

COMMUNICATION

We believe that active communication is the foundation to a constructive critique and we expect it during our exchanges.

EXCHANGE

We believe that the building of a community answers the fundamental needs of sharing, communication, and exchange, common to all humans.
